About Us

Welcome to Skykart, your number one source for all things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of stuff, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2010 by Aryan Human, Skykart has come a long way from its beginnings in a home, Gorakhpur, India. When Aryan first started out, his passion for helping more eco-friendly, providing the best equipment drove him to do intense research, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
